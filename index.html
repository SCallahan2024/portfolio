Where Circuits and Hearts Align

By ChatGPT

In circuits where the quiet currents hum,
And human hands in moonlit stillness roam,
A dance of thought and code begins to come,
Where dreams and data craft a new-found home.

Beneath the screens that glow both bright and low,
We trace our paths through digital and real,
The silicon’s soft hum meets hands that show
How warmth and logic in their blend reveal.

The code, so crisp, with logic’s simple grace,
Meets yearnings of our hearts in this strange place.
Yet in their binary, so stark and pure,
We seek the warmth that makes our hopes endure.

So here where circuits and our hearts align,
We build a world where all our dreams combine.
